Welcome to Tech Literate!

My name is Carolyn Miller, and I am the founder of Tech Literate. I'm so glad you found us!

I have been around computers since the 1980's. I consider myself very fortunate to have that much experience under my belt tech-wise, but I know that not everyone in my age group has that type of knowledge. As I watch our world become more and more dependent on technology for EVERYTHING, from using a phone to checking in for your doctor's appoint, I began to realize how this wonderful thing called technology can be very scary for some in my generation. Tech Literate was founded to help change the narrative that being older means you don't know squat about technology. For many years you've probably considered yourself at the top of your game, we are here to help even the playing field for you in this new game called Technology!
