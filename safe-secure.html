Cyber Security Tips

Don't Help the Grinch Steal Your Christmas

Tip 1:

Stay safe, stay confident—your digital life matters.

Be skeptical of unexpected messages. Don’t click on links or open attachments from unfamiliar senders. Even if a message looks like it’s from a friend or company, double-check—scammers often impersonate trusted sources.

Tip 2

Pause before acting on urgency:

Scammers use panic to pressure you. If someone says “act now” or “don’t tell anyone,” stop and talk to someone you trust.

Tip 3

Learn the common scams:

Watch out for the following: “Sweetheart” scams (fake online romances).Tech support scams (fake pop-ups or calls). Fake government benefits or debt relief offers. Impersonation of customer service reps.
